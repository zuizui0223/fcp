## Fields intentionally not inferred

The following remain unverified and must be confirmed by the author group:

- final author order;
- any additional authors;
- laboratory or room number;
- corresponding-author designation;
- ORCID;
- telephone number;
- CRediT roles;
- funding and grant numbers;
- conflicts of interest;
- acknowledgements and biosketch.

A Web of Science ResearcherID found in mailbox records is not an ORCID and was not entered. A search for an ORCID linked to the verified institutional email returned no result. Absence from that search does not establish that the author has no ORCID.

## Privacy and audit boundary

- Private email bodies, personal document contents and message identifiers are not copied into this repository.
- The public repository records only the minimum verified metadata required to prepare the submission.
- Final approval must be recorded in `docs/jbi_author_confirmation_form.md`.
- `validate_jbi_author_prefill.py` protects the verified spelling, division, institution, general postal address and institutional email while rejecting unverified ORCID-like values, additional email addresses or silent corresponding-author assignment.
